About Me

I am Nawab Amedi, a second-year software engineering student at the University of Ottawa. I am ambitious and consistently working to improve my skills in both software development and design. Outside of academics, I enjoy playing sports to stay active, and I also play video games, which has helped me develop problem-solving skills and quick decision-making.

How I Work

I do not currently have professional experience in UI design. At the moment, I am taking a UI design course where I am learning the fundamentals of interface design, including layout, color theory, accessibility, and user-centered design principles.

I am also using industry resources such as the Nielsen Norman Group (NN/g) to better understand established usability standards and best practices.

My current design process is focused on learning and improvement. It involves understanding basic concepts, applying them in simple designs, receiving feedback, and refining my work. As the course progresses, I will continue developing my understanding of concepts such as user-centered design, heuristic evaluation, and iterative design.
